W.E.C - the World Exploration Center

Welcome to W.E.C, the World Exploration Center. Here at W.E.C, we want to make sure that every child has a hands-on experiance to learn. At W.E.C, we design kid-friendly activities and posters that enable every child to learn about the world around them. W.E.C's main goal is to make learning fun for everyone. W.E.C employees make sure W.E.C is fun for kids, since it is desined by kids! Make an appointment or call today!
